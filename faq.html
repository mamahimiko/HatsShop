FAQ

What sizes do your hats come in?

We offer a variety of sizes including Small, Medium, Large, and Adjustable/Flexible fits. Each product page includes a size chart to help you find the perfect fit.

How do I measure my head for the right hat size

Use a soft measuring tape and wrap it around the widest part of your head (just above the ears and eyebrows). Compare your measurement to our size chart on the product page.

Do you offer free shipping

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over €50 within [your region/country]. Express shipping is also available at checkout for an additional fee.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Standard shipping typically takes 3–5 business days within [your region/country]. International shipping may take 7–14 business days depending on customs.

Can I return or exchange a hat if it doesn’t fit?

Absolutely. You can return or exchange unworn, unwashed hats within 30 days of delivery. Please check our Returns & Exchanges page for full details.

How do I care for my hat to keep it looking new?

Spot-clean your hat with a damp cloth and mild soap. Avoid machine washing unless the product page says it’s safe. Store your hat in a cool, dry place to maintain its shape.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and times will vary depending on your location.

Are your hats sustainable or ethically made?

We’re committed to responsible sourcing. Many of our hats are made from recycled or organic materials, and we work with suppliers who follow ethical labor practices.

Can I customize or personalize a hat?

Yes! We offer embroidery and color customization options on select styles. Look for the “Customize” option on the product page or contact our customer service team.
